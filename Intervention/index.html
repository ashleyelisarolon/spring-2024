Intervention Reflection

Reddit is a discussion website where users can post and discuss links, images, and videos on various topics, including news, entertainment, technology, and more.
What interests me about Reddit is its unique upvoting and downvoting system, allowing users to vote on the quality of content, making it easier to find the most valuable and
engaging content. My intervention was a collaborative mural with the theme of "Metamorphosis." The aim is to encourage artists to interpret the theme uniquely while
suggesting they incorporate vibrating greens. The top 25 most upvoted comments would then be featured in the collaborative mural. It was officially established on March
10th, 2024. I am still waiting to receive feedback on this intervention. It was expected because it was done recently, and Reddit has many different subreddits; therefore, it
could take a while before it gets any attention. If I had more time to expand on my intervention, I would explore different ways to promote it. One approach would be to reach
out to art-focused subreddits and communities to encourage participation and engagement with the project. I could also partner with other artists or organizations to help
spread the word and attract a wider audience.
